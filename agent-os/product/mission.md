# Product Mission

## Problem

Travelers to Yemen lack a modern, localized hotel booking platform. Existing solutions don't support Arabic language properly, don't understand the local market, and can't manage multiple hotel branches efficiently.

## Target Users

**Primary:** Travelers to Yemen — tourists, business travelers, and locals seeking hotel accommodations across three hotel branches in different cities.

**Secondary:** Hotel staff and administrators managing reservations, rooms, pricing, and guest communications.

## Solution

A bilingual (English/Arabic) hotel booking system with:

- **Full RTL support** — Native Arabic experience with proper layout transformation
- **Yemen market focus** — Built specifically for local hotel operations
- **Multi-branch management** — Unified system for 3 hotel locations with branch-specific configuration
- **Complete booking workflow** — Search, book, manage, and review stays
- **Comprehensive admin tools** — Inventory, pricing, guest management, and analytics
